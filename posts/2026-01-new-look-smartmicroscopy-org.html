New look smartmicroscopy.org!

We have updated the website smartmicroscopy.org! To make more open source, we have moved away from wordpress and now the website's backbone can be found on GitHub.

You may also notice that the website redirects you to smartmicroscopy.nl. This is temporary, and we will be back to .org soon enough!

As always, fee fre to send us any comments you may have for the website, either through the contact form or in the github.
